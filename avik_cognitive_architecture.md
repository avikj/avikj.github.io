**The mass analogy is precise:**
- Physical spacetime: Mass/energy → curvature → geodesics
- Cognitive spacetime: Desire/aversion → curvature → stream of consciousness

Dense masses of desire/aversion create strong local curvature, dominating geodesic structure. Rumination = **trapped in deep gravitational well** of desire or aversion, unable to escape high-curvature region.

**These systems use different "languages"**—different encodings, different time scales, different access to consciousness. They evolved separately and communicate imperfectly.

**Cognitive dissonance = translation failure** between domains. Verbal processing says "this is fine" while somatic processing signals "danger"—but conscious mind can't directly read somatic encoding, only feels vague unease (negative sense).
